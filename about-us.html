3S Culture and Values

Founded in 2006, 3S is a fast growing IT Outsourcing Global Services Company. With offices in the US, Canada and India, a rapidly growing client base, strategic partnerships and healthy financials, 3S is quickly emerging as a global player in the IT space.

3S provides world-class technology, services and solutions that are designed to help your company improve operations, sales, service and customer loyalty.

Our mission is to add value to your enterprise while working within your budget and schedule. We constantly explore and implement innovative solutions that drive long-term value to our customers and go beyond simply meeting your technology needs. We collaborate with you to provide innovative, high quality, cost-effective IT-based solutions and services, making your company more efficient and profitable.

Our experienced IT professionals work with multiple platforms in many verticals, earning your trust with their strong project-management skills, accessibility and dedication. Our innovative StrategicShoring™ model facilitates integrated services, quick delivery, low pricing and high quality.

We strive to improve the flow and management of your data enabling a strong bottom line for you. Known for its commitment to quality processes and performance, 3S is 9001:2000 compliant to ensure consistent results, and is on track towards receiving its CMMI Level 3 certification.

All our strategic offerings include:

  • Unequivocal commitment to Stakeholders
  • Dedicated Project Management Office (PMO)
  • Highly qualified technical experts drawn from some of the best global technology institutes & IT firms
  • Unique delivery model using an optimal combination of local project management and global resources
  • State-of-the-art, worldwide infrastructure with secure network and sophisticated collaborative technologies
  • Commitment to best practices and standards (ISO, CMMI) focused on solving problems and exceeding client's business objectives
  • Financial strength and stability

The 3S Advantage

3S is an enterprise that is dedicated to engaging its customers as partners, proactively solving problems, and delivering the right service, at the right time, at the right place, and in the right way, continuously raising the bar for service excellence.

There are several things which we do differently at 3S. We take great pride in having delivered high-quality projects on-time, and on budget. We are differentiated by a methodical approach to project management, our 360 degree Customer Focus, the way we build and nurture our talent pools, and the way we do business with our clients.

We utilize standard tools and methodologies which bring additional cost benefits for the customer apart from an On-Time, with-in Budget & Zero Defect Delivery Experience..... What we term as THE "3S Experience".

  • StrategicShoring™ - Our unique StrategicShoring™ delivery model offers customers the benefit of "RIGHT" global sourcing delivery methodology that provides reliability and value.

  • True Partner Approach - When we engage, we engage as a partner. We raise the red flag when we see things off track; we share our recommendations proactively while maintaining an extremely transparent delivery environment. This aspect is apparent where even the most junior team member is personally invested in the ownership of the work he is involved in. It is this commitment and ownership that significantly differentiates us in the industry and which gives that BIG push to the quality and timeliness of the work we do.

  • Technology Enabled Services - We invest in and reuse our Core Platform (Framework) to deliver repeatable and scalable solutions in short timeframes. We believe that technology is an enabler and it has to complement the business well. So, with our Wide Technology Competence and unique Rapid Prototyping approach we strive to analyze and understand your business and recommend a solution that is best-suited for your specific environment while in the longer term provides a lower total-cost-of-ownership.

  • Proven Methodologies & Domain Expertise - We emphasize requirements-acquisition, effective project-management and development models, e.g. Project Management Office (PMO) and Virtual Centers of Excellence (VCE).
